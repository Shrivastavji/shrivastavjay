The Toyota Supra Mk4 is a formidable beast and the JZ engine just keeps on going thanks to Toyota’s first class engineering. Many Toyota cars run great after 20+ years,
not just the Supra. This is thanks to Toyota actually overbuilding their engines, meaning that parts are made thicker unnecessarily to allow for better engine health,
performance, and reliability. Toyota’s JZ engine was so good, that they used it in all kinds of different cars,
like the Toyota Soarer and Chaser, proving the engine’s pedigree, and showing that the Supra was bred for performance.
The engine used in the Supra MK4 is such a powerful engine, that it is often used for other car models for modification and performance.
